# TinyMal asset notice

The TinyMal URDF and STL meshes were supplied with the 2026 Bionic and Humanoid Robotics course materials. The URDF header identifies the SolidWorks-to-URDF exporter, but the supplied asset bundle does not include an explicit copyright owner or redistribution license for the robot model.

These assets are retained in the initial private ActuateX repository solely to reproduce the experiments. Before changing the repository to public, obtain permission, add the correct license and attribution, or replace the model with a redistributable equivalent.
